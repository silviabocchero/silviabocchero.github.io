Hi!

I started to create my own world made of words, tales and illustrations since I was a little child, and learned later how to give it the right shape through Graphic Design. I explored the infinite fields of Graphic Design getting fascinated by EVERYTHING. But my big love is still illustration.

I believe in people.
I believe in travelling as a tool of knowledge, and I dream to apply illustration to social projects using design as an unexpected way to improve people’s life.

The Boring Part, if ya want.
I graduated with honors in 2017 at Naba (Milan) in Graphic Design & Art Direction, with a thesis on Graphic Journalism and the possibilities of AR applied to publishing. Wanting to connect my graphic skills to the digital world, I’m currently enrolled on a Master Degree in Web and Digital Design at SPD (Scuola Politecnica di Design) + Iulm (International University of Languages and Media), in Milan.
