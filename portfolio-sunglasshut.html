00. The project

  • Research
  • Website Structure
  • Website Prototype

Rebranding of sunglasshut.com, that will become the curator of the best brands & the incubator of unique product stories. One of the key ingredient to reach this goal is to provide curated content, contextualizing the different products the website sells.

One of the most challenging part the team dealt with is researching on what "luxury" means today, improving the User Experience in the e-commerce and translating these concept into a successful design.

01. Research:

COMPETITORS BENCHMARKING

Look at the storytelling strategies of e-commerce mavericks.

Leverage sector intelligence from eyewear conglomerates.

WHAT DEFINES A PREMIUM PERCEPTION?

Time
Opportunities for presenting updated product stock / collections raises premium perception by drawing attention to SGH as an e-commerce aggregator brand. Numbers and content that mark change also convey a perception of timeliness.

Typography
More gender neutral and refined typographic choices can help reposition the brand by balancing feminine and residual

Brand Curation
Premium players in E-Commerce are engaging as expert brand curators unique product stories. One of the key ingredient to reach this goal is to provide curated content, contextualizing the different products the website sells, instead of simply listing them over a white limbo.
