THE TRUMPET WEBSITE

Web Design / Digital Publishing / Typography

Creation of a strong visual identity for the luxury e-commerce The Trumpet. Naming, logo, website (e- commerce structure + editorial content + design).

Corporate Identity

NAMING & LOGO

The condensed look and delicate letters make the logo high-class and neat. The extreme extended letters recalls the image of a trumpet, with its long and neat elements. There’s an additional delicate reference to the trumpet’s bell in the upper-case “T”.

The pictogram it’s designed by playing with “the Trumpet” letters, selecting the most representative ones: the “t”s.

SPACE-AREA & PALETTE

FONTS COMBINATIONS

Create a visual connection with the condensed logo’s font. Readable fonts + contrast: light/bold, serif/sans-serif, classy/modern.

THE MANIFESTO

The colours are bright, eccessive, complementary; they create an almost shocking effect. Together they have a strong personality. The sections seem to overlap. “Modern manifesto” hyphenation is intentionally wrong, to play with words and the concept of being unconventional, breaking all rules.
